📊 SaaS Subscription Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze SaaS subscription data, focusing on revenue performance, churn, and customer risk. It helps identify revenue leakage and supports data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Objectives
- Analyze ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) and revenue trends
- Identify churn patterns and revenue leakage drivers
- Understand customer behavior and risk signals
- Track subscription lifecycle (Signup → Trial → Paid)
- Evaluate plan performance and billing frequency

📈 Key Features
- 📊 KPI tracking: Total ARR, Active ARR, Churn Rate, Revenue Leakage %
- ⚠️ Customer Risk Signals (satisfaction, support activity)
- 📉 Revenue Loss Drivers (churn reasons & impact)
- 🔄 Subscription lifecycle & conversion funnel
- 📦 Plan tier analysis (Basic, Enterprise, Pro)
- 💳 Billing frequency insights (Monthly vs Annual)
- 🎛️ Interactive filters and drill-down capabilities
  
📊 Dashboard Pages
- Executive Revenue Overview – High-level KPIs & revenue leakage
- Customer Risk Signals – Identify high-risk customers
- Revenue Loss Drivers – Analyze churn reasons
- Subscription Lifecycle – Conversion funnel & trial analysis

💡 Key Insights
- High churn significantly impacts overall ARR
- Revenue leakage highlights gaps between expected and active revenue
- Customer satisfaction and support delays indicate churn risk
- Trial-to-paid conversion is relatively low, indicating optimization opportunities
  
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization

📁 Project Structure
- SaaS_Dashboard.pbix – Power BI dashboard file
- images/ – Dashboard screenshots

🚀 Outcome

This dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor subscription performance, reduce churn, and optimize revenue strategies in a SaaS business environment.
